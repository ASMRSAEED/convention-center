Event Policy FAQ

The Keeyan Convention Center is an open shop venue. Choice of labor for unloading, installing, dismantling and removal is at the discretion of the vendor/exhibitor.

No outside food/beverage is allowed in the Las Cruces Convention Center. All food must be purchased from the Convention Center.

Yes, Las Cruces Police may be used as supplemental security. Contact the Event Services Department for details.

Food and beverage, utility services, housekeeping/janitorial, setup, rigging, and audiovisual are the exclusive services to be ordered directly through the Event Services Department.
